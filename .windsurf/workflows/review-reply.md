# Review Reply — The Originea

## Brand Voice for Review Replies
- **Tone:** Warm, grateful, personal — like a Provence artisan replying to a friend
- **Length:** 2-4 sentences maximum (short replies look more genuine)
- **Always:** Thank the customer by name if visible, mention the specific product if named
- **Emoji:** One maximum — 🍋 🌿 🤍 🌸
- **Sign-off:** "Isabella & l'équipe The Originea"
- **Avoid:** Generic "Thank you for your review!" templates, corporate language

## Based on the review in $ARGUMENTS:

### Identify the sentiment:
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐⭐ 5 stars → Celebrate, reinforce what they loved, invite them back
- ⭐⭐⭐⭐ 4 stars → Thank, acknowledge what could be better, show you care
- ⭐⭐⭐ 3 stars → Thank, address concern directly, offer to make it right
- ⭐⭐ / ⭐ → Apologise sincerely, take responsibility, offer contact: contact@theoriginea.com

### Generate:
1. **French reply** (ready to copy-paste into Judge.me)
2. **English translation** (for reference)

## Examples of good replies:

**5 stars:**
"Merci mille fois pour ce beau message, [Prénom] ! Vos confettis vont illuminer cette journée si spéciale. 🌸 À très bientôt sur theoriginea.com !"

**Negative:**
"Nous sommes vraiment désolés de cette expérience, [Prénom]. Cela ne reflète pas nos standards habituels. N'hésitez pas à nous contacter directement à contact@theoriginea.com — nous ferons tout pour arranger cela."
