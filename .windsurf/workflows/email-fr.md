# Customer Email — The Originea Brand Voice

## Brand Voice for Emails
- **Tone:** Warm, personal, artisanal — like a letter from a small Provence producer
- **Salutation:** "Bonjour [Prénom]," (never "Cher/Chère client(e)")
- **Register:** Vouvoiement (vous) throughout
- **Sign-off:** "Isabella\nThe Originea — Artisanal Products France"
- **Emoji:** Use sparingly — 🍋 🌿 🤍 are on-brand
- **Avoid:** Corporate language, excessive exclamation marks, English words

## Based on $ARGUMENTS, write an email for:

Common situations:
- **order-delay** → Apologise for delay, give updated timeline, offer goodwill gesture
- **review-request** → Ask for a Shopify review, include product link
- **wholesale-inquiry** → Respond to B2B/restaurant/hotel inquiry
- **restock** → Notify customer a product is back in stock
- **welcome** → Welcome a new subscriber or first-time customer
- **custom** → Write based on the situation described in $ARGUMENTS

## Output format:
1. **Subject line** (French, max 60 chars)
2. **Preview text** (French, max 90 chars)
3. **Email body** (French, ready to send)
4. **English translation** (for reference)
